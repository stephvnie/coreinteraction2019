What We Mean When We Say “responsive”
Responsive web design is an approach whereby a designer creates a web page that
“responds to” or resizes itself depending on the type of device it is being seen through.
it is a way of developing website through different properties so that the device on determines
the way that the site will be displayed. so when the screen is being resized the website gives out certain
responses to suit the window/screen better.
